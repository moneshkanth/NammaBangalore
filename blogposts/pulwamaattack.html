Pulwama Attack - 40 CRPF Soldiers Dead

  • Tasneem Fathima
  • 15 February 2019
  • 0
  • 46
Share Tweet Pin Email

Unfortunate day - entire nation speaks in one voice

An unexpectable incident took place at Awantipora in Jammu and Kashmir’s Pulwama district on Thursday which forced Indians to raise voice against terrorists. There’s a lot of anger in the nation, the blood of the nation is boiling. There was a deep sadness and anger to know about the cowardly attack on CRPF jawans convoy in Pulwama.

When a 78 – vehicle convoy was escorting over 2,500 Central Reserve Police Force(CRPF) from Jammu to Srinagar. A Mahindra Scorpio SUV, which was packed by more than 350 kilograms of explosives, crashed one of the bus convoy on the national highway 44 at Lethipora near Avantipora town. The blast within the fraction of second blew up in the air, which killed more than 40 jawans. The rest of them who got luckily saved struggled for their lives and rushed towards the army base hospital in Srinagar.

According to the terror outfit the attack was carried by a 20 years old suicide bomber named Asil Ahamed Dar, who crashed the car into bus. Suicide bomber’s parents gave a complaint that he went missing when he at class 12. Some of the initial reporters claimed that the attack was triggered by an IED explosion, later they concluded that it was Dar who was behind the Pulwama attack.

The death of the great Jawans turned the hearts of Indians in a deep sorrow. Some daughters were waiting to play with father, one of the wives was eager to show their child who was just few days old, mothers were restlessly waiting to see their child. In this way every family have their own emotions and feelings which were broken into pieces which cannot be fixed again. The whole country felt very sad and their heart started to burn towards the attack. Even though the intension of terror attack was to divide our country, the whole nation should together and decided to support the family members of jawans.

Once again, a great salute for all the CRPF jawans for being the bravest of all who sacrificed their lives for the nation. Remember one thing if they are not at borders struggling for our safety we might not have slept peacefully. And a big round of applause to their courageous families who sent their loved ones to protect the people in this nation. May god give peace to their souls and wishing the injured to recover.

Our deep condolences for the families who lost their loved ones
