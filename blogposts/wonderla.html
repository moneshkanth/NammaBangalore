Wonderla

  • Tasneem Fathima
  • 22 February 2019
  • 0
  • 46
Share Tweet Pin Email

Delightful experience of enjoyment !

wonderla an place where you can have lots of fun and play lots of games. This is a heaven for the kids and youth where they can play unlimitedly irrespective of tiredness both in land and as well as in water. This is the best place to get thrilled and spend your time with family and friends .

WELCOME TO WONDER LA

This is not only an amusement part but a breath-taking charm, which has more than 60+ exhibiting rides. Some are thrilling, while some are funny and there are spots to get relaxed. There is no better place where you can spend time and gather the sweet memories. The rides are categorised into Land rides, Water rides, High thrill rides and Kids rides, u can ride as per your preferences.

EXPERIENCE A RIDE BY SITTING STABLE:

Adventure of Chikku is a place where u sit in theatre and by wearing 3D glasses you will experience crazy rides, you can feel the movement of your feet, splashing of water, fear. The Musical fountain and laser show make you not to take your eyes off, where water dances in the music with colourful LED’s

ADVENTUROUS RIDES:

There are also adventurous rides which makes you feel dizzy like Flung Glides, Pirate Ship, Techno jump, Roller coaster. Don’t forget to visit net walk, sky wheel, toon tango, dungeon ride, crazy cars, crazy wagon.

MUSIC+DISCO+DANCE:

The most important thing that flashes in your mind when you think about water games is rain disco, where you dance in the crowd leaving all the worries and involve deeper to the rain disco..

The another most thrilling ride that should not be missed is slides where it suddenly drops you from the top to bottom with high speed and up and down curves. This type of feeling can be experienced by Jungle Lagoon, Boomerang, Uphill racers, Bandel Kraits, Water Pendulum. Lazy river, Fun racers, Water Pools, Play Pools are the rides where you can relax by floating on the water under the sun.

CRAZIEST !! LOUDEST!! THRILLING!! RIDES:

If you are dare enough to experience the continuous lub-dub lub-dub sound which increases your heartbeat then these rides are best if you love adventure and just try it once, surely you are not going to forget these rides. During the rides you open your mouth and shout in a volume where your vocal chords gets spoiled. Few rides are • FLASH TOWER: shoots up and suddenly falls which creates a feeling of falling from the top. • EQUINOX: ride which is outside seated and moves circularly which makes you feel drowsy. • DROP ZONE • WHY SCREAM • WONDERLA BAMBA • INSANITY • HURRICANE

RIDES LOVED BY UNTAINTED HEARTS:

Kids love to play. Even if you leave them for whole day, they play until they get tired. Children are the pure form of love. So, wonder la didn’t miss the rides for game lovers. The rides offered for children are • MAGIC MUSHROOM: it is just like a ferry wheel but for a change we can see bees and flies moving around a mushroom. • Mini Venice: a small express going in and out of tunnel which looks like Venice tunnel. • MOON WALKER: a wavy slide where little children can slide over. • MERRY GHOST • KIDDIES WHEEL • MINI PIRATE SHIP • MINI EXPRESS

ENJOYMENT FOR FOODIES:

The restaurants will surely make you to fall in love with there dishes according to your taste preferences • CHILLIES RESTAURANT: speciality for spicy foods • GREEN RESTAURANT: for purely vegetarians • WAVES RESTAURANT: north Indian foods • COUNTRYARD RESTAURANT: for all types of foods • CRAZY PIZZA: where you can get various varities of pizzas

TIPS FOR A PERFECT ENJOYMENT:

• Wear only nylon dresses and shorts • Don’t wear long dresses, dupattas • Always be with group • Don’t waste time by spending to take photos because you may miss rides due to lack of time

FACILITES

• You can skip the long queue and get the rides faster by Fastrack • You can find your lost belongings at lost and found counters • Lockers, wheel chairs, ex pay facilities • For emergencies, there are well equipped first-aid

PARK TIMINGS:

• Normal season weekdays:11:00AM to 6:00PM • Peak season:11:00AM to 7:00PM • Water park timings on normal season weekdays:12:30PM to 5:00PM • Water park timings on peak seasons: 12:00PM to 6:00AM

Approx ₹ 1000 - 2000 for one & couple ₹ 2000 - 3500

prices may vary according to the days
