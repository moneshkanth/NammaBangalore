A Glimpse into Bengaluru flower market

K.R.Market widely known as city market is one of the major attractions and also known as the largest wholesale market dealing with the wholesale commodities in Bangalore, India.

A new concrete 3-story structure was built between the two older buildings to provide more space for flower vendors and better overall conditions. At the basement is the underground parking above which stand three commodity-specialised floors: flowers and vegetables on the lower ground floor, dry goods on the upper ground floor and tools and machine-tools accessories on the first floor.

This market is a busy, vibrant and crowded market in the heart of the city. Go early in the morning to watch fresh vegetables and flowers arrive for the day. A snapper’s delight, this market gets covered up with a crowded madness regularly and especially before important religious festivals. The best time to visit is before sunrise when things are at their busiest. The sights and smells are overwhelming and it's a great place to get a cup of tea and watch the flower sellers and buyers do their things.

Just imagine becoming the way you used to be as a very young child, before you understood the meaning of any word, before opinions took over your mind. The real you is loving, joyful, and free. The real you is just like a flower, just like the wind, just like the ocean, just like the sun.

Things to do in this market:

• Largest market in the asia for flowers. • Wholesale market for groceries and flowers spices and More. • Mouthwatering street foods with a wide variety of foods, dishes and covered with exotic flavours. • You can bag up Electronic gadgets for affordable prices. • Visit the Sunday market to get all appliances for reasonable prices
